# AI & Blockchain

## Overview
The convergence of AI and blockchain will redefine trust,
transparency, and automation in the digital world.

## Key Predictions

- **Smart Contracts 2.0** — AI will write, audit, and execute
  smart contracts autonomously without human intervention
- **Fraud Detection** — AI will monitor blockchain transactions
  in real-time, eliminating fraud before it happens
- **Decentralized AI** — AI models will run on blockchain networks,
  making them censorship-resistant and community-owned
- **AI-Powered DAOs** — Decentralized Autonomous Organizations
  will be governed by AI agents, not humans
- **Supply Chain** — AI + blockchain will create fully transparent,
  self-optimizing global supply chains
- **Digital Identity** — AI will manage blockchain-based identities,
  replacing passwords and traditional authentication
- **Token Economics** — AI will dynamically adjust token supply
  and demand to stabilize crypto markets

## Conclusion
AI and blockchain together will create a decentralized, autonomous,
and trustless future — removing middlemen from almost every industry.
